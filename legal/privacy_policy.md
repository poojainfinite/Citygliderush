## 1. Information We Collect

**City Glide: 3-Lane Rush does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

- No user accounts or login required
- No name, email, phone, or address collected
- No location, camera, microphone, or contacts access
- No photos, files, or external storage access
- All game progress is saved **locally** on your device only

## 2. Platform / Device Compatibility

- The app is designed for Android devices and should be packaged for production with **targetSdkVersion 35** (Android 15) and **minSdkVersion 21 or higher**.
- If native Android packaging includes third-party native libraries, those libraries should support **16 KB page sizes** on Android 15+ devices.
- In-app purchases, if enabled in the Android app package, should be processed through **Google Play Billing Library v7 or newer**.

## 3. Third-Party Services

### Advertising (AdMob by Google)
- This app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob
- AdMob may collect and process:
  - Device identifiers (Advertising ID, IDFV)
  - Approximate location (country/city level, not GPS)
  - App usage data for ad personalization and measurement
  - Crash and performance data
- This data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Users can manage ad personalization in their device settings

### In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)
- Purchases are processed by Google Play
- We do not store payment information
- Google Play handles all transaction data per their privacy policy

## 4. Children's Privacy

This app is **NOT directed to children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us at citygliderush.app@gmail.com.

## 5. Your Rights

- **Access/Deletion:** Since we store no personal data, there is nothing to access or delete from our side
- **Device Settings:** You can reset your Advertising ID in Android Settings > Google > Ads
- **Opt Out:** You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

## 6. Data Retention

- Local game data: Stored until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- AdMob data: Governed by Google's data retention policies

## 7. Security

All third-party communications use HTTPS encryption. No data is transmitted from the app to our servers (we have no servers).
